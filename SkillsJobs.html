Mobile Software Engineer

About Us

Multicultural Tech Co. is a diverse and vibrant new startup company looking to grow and expand. We are a small but tight-knit team focused on creating and innovating tech products to make peoples’ lives easier. We are currently in the process of creating a new software to help people all over the country with one of the most frustrating daily tasks: finding a parking spot.

We believe working for Multicultural Tech Co. is not just a job but a purpose. Working with us you will get to make real change in the world and aid in making this company one of the leading innovators in tech. The name says it all, but we are an accepting and welcoming team where everyone can embrace their uniqueness. If this sounds like a place you’d like to be then please do not hesitate to apply.

The Role

This role will see you as a front end developer for our mobile app which allows customers to interact with our software. You will coordinate with both the product manager and designer to create an application which is smooth and user friendly. Additionally, you will communicate with our quality assurance team to solve any bugs and keep the application running smoothly.

Being that our team is so small currently, this job will entail more responsibility compared to an average software engineering job. You will be the first point of contact for anything software related and will lead the direction for our company in this field. You will be able to make a real impact at Multicultural Tech Co. and have a seat at the table to suggest ideas and improvements.

About You

You will be a skilled and experienced mobile software engineer, but more importantly you will have the right attitude and be a team player. As you will be the first software engineer that we hire, it would be desirable for you to also have managerial skills and experience because as our company grows you will likely move up the chain and lead your own team.

Specific Requirements:

- React Native/C#

- APIs and integrations

- Knowldge of all aspects of the mobile app development process

Desirables:

- Experience with Swift/Kotlin/Java etc.

- Managerial experience and skills

- Thrive in a team environment

- Excellent organisational skills

- Bachelor’s Degree in Software Engineering or related field
