For an example Georgie and Gazi both are looking to obtain a job in the IT field, yet because of how extensive and large the IT field is, Georgies job relates to programming, whilst Gazis relates more to managing databases, showing that although two jobs or in our groups cases multiple jobs are within the same category they are still widely different from one another. This then correlates into showing how similar yet different everyone's careers plans are, with the clear differences being the people who chose jobs out of the IT field, but this also reveals the clear differences that can be shown for the remainder of the group who all chose jobs in the IT field.
