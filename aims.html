Aims

Overall aim of our project

The overall aim of our project is to develop a software that can help the users to determine empty parking spots around a vicinity. The main aim behind the software that we are developing is to make the lives of both the drivers and the Traffic control department a little easier. We are hoping that through the help of our software:

  • The drivers will be able to save money, fuel and time from driving around aimlessly looking for parking spots in a congested area.
  • The drivers will be able to Save 15-20 minutes of their day by not leaving their homes early in order to search for parking spots.
  • They will also be able to avoid congested areas to park which will be more convenient for a far more smoother drive and less chances of collision. This will also result in helping the traffic control department and the government.
